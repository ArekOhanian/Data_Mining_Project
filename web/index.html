Framework Insights
TARGETING
Behavioral Segmentation
Use cluster profiles to target campaigns by demographic and spend behavior. High-age + high-spend clusters respond to premium positioning; younger clusters to value-driven messaging.
EMAIL
Personalized Outreach
Craft cluster-specific email sequences. Segment by recency and rating — low-rating clusters need re-engagement content; high-rating clusters are candidates for loyalty programs.
OFFERS
Dynamic Promotions
Match discount depth to cluster spend sensitivity. Price-elastic clusters convert on flash sales; premium clusters prefer exclusive access and early product releases.
RETENTION
Lifecycle Automation
Set up triggered workflows per cluster: onboarding sequences for new customers, win-back flows for dormant segments, and VIP programs for top-spending cohorts.
GROWTH
Upsell Mapping
Identify clusters with high review ratings but moderate spend — these are upsell-ready customers. Present bundle offers and premium SKUs to accelerate their LTV.
RISK
Churn Prevention
Clusters with low review ratings and below-average spend signal at-risk customers. Prioritize proactive outreach, feedback loops, and satisfaction incentives.
